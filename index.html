Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about InvestTrack

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Do I need to connect my bank accounts?

No, connecting bank accounts is optional. You can manually add and track your investments without linking any external accounts.

Can I track investments from multiple brokerages?

Yes, InvestTrack allows you to aggregate investments from multiple sources, giving you a comprehensive view of your entire portfolio.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, we offer mobile apps for both iOS and Android platforms, allowing you to track your investments on the go.
